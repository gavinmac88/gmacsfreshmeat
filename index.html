About us

Est. 2023 · Muskoka, Ontario

Gmac's Fresh Meat was started in 2023 with a simple passion: bringing fresh, honest, Ontario-raised meat directly to the people of Muskoka. What began as a farmers market stall has grown into a community staple — and in 2026, we're excited to offer home delivery for the first time.

Every product we sell is grass-fed and finished beef, non-GMO chicken, or sourced from trusted Ontario farms. No mystery, no middlemen — just quality meat at a fair price, from our family to yours.
