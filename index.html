About us

Est. 2023 · Muskoka, Ontario

Gmac's Fresh Meat was started in 2023 as a summer business when i was at my first year at western university. with a simple passion: bringing fresh, honest, Ontario-raised meat directly to the Farmers Markets of Muskoka.

Every product we sell is grass-fed and finished beef, non-GMO chicken, or sourced from trusted Ontario farms. No mystery, just quality meat at a fair price, brought to you in cottage country.
