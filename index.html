About Us

Est. 2023 · Muskoka, Ontario

Gmac's Fresh Meat was started in 2023 as a summer business when I was in my first year at Western University, with a simple passion: bringing fresh, honest, Ontario-raised meat directly to the farmers markets of Muskoka.

Every product we sell is grass-fed and finished beef, non-GMO chicken, or sourced from trusted Ontario farms. No mystery — just quality meat at a fair price, brought to you in cottage country.
